# Executive Summary

**Client:** SDSC SHAR (Satish Dhawan Space Centre SHAR)
**Tender Ref:** Supply of Copper cables (No specific tender number provided)
**Submission Mode:** Online via GeM (Two-part bid system)

## Critical Dates
*   **Submission Deadline:** None
*   **Opening Date:** None

## Scope of Work
The scope of work is the supply of Armoured PIJF copper cables (10P, 20P, 50P, 100P) and PVC switch board copper cables (5P) to SDSC SHAR for telecommunications purposes. The procurement is based on a two-part bid system (Techno-Commercial and Price Bid) submitted online via the GeM portal.

**Estimated Value:** Not Disclosed

## Key Risks & Flags
*   Mandatory compliance with Public Procurement (Preference to Make in India) Order, requiring Class-I local supplier status (>=50% local content).
*   Bidder must be the Original Equipment Manufacturer (OEM) or its authorized distributor.
*   Strict delivery schedule of 8 weeks from the date of order placement.
*   Liquidated Damages (LD) of 0.5% per week, up to 10% of the undelivered item value.
*   Order can be split based on Individual Item wise L1 evaluation.
*   Requires 3% Performance Bank Guarantee (PBG) and 3% Security Deposit (SD).
