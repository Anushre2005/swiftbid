# Compliance & Eligibility Checklist

## Eligibility Criteria
- [ ] **Class Requirement:** Class-I Local Supplier (>=50% local content)
- [ ] **Local Content Requirement:** 50.0% 
- [ ] **Turnover:** Minimum 100 Lakhs for the past three years ending with March 2024.
- [ ] **Blacklisting Declaration:** Required

## Experience Criteria
**Summary:** Prior experience in supplying Telecom Copper cables to Government/ Public organizations/ Reputed MNC's in the last 3 years ending with March 2024. Documentary evidence (P.O. copies, acceptance/completion report/ user satisfaction certificate) must be submitted.
- [ ] Single order value > 100 Lakhs (Min Value: 100.0 Lakhs)
- [ ] Two orders, each > 75 Lakhs (Min Value: 75.0 Lakhs)
- [ ] Three orders, each > 50 Lakhs (Min Value: 50.0 Lakhs)

## Required Documents
- [ ] Bid Letter (signed and stamped)
- [ ] Technical Compliance Statement and Technical Data Sheets/Test Certificates for all cable types
- [ ] Product Brochures/Documentation
- [ ] Self-declaration of being OEM or OEM Authorization Certificate (if distributor)
- [ ] Self-declaration of not being blacklisted by any Govt. Department/PSU
- [ ] Affidavit regarding non-use of blacklisted 3rd party manufacturers (as per GFR2017 Clause 144)
- [ ] Self-certification of Make in India (MII) content (>=50%)
- [ ] Proof of OEM manufacturing copper cables for the last 5 years (ending March 2024)
- [ ] Performance Bank Guarantee (PBG) of 3% of total value
- [ ] Security Deposit (SD) of 3% of contract price
