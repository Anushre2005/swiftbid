# Commercial & Logistics Terms

## Delivery & Incoterms
*   **Incoterms:** F.O.R. Destination Basis (SRIHARIKOTA)
*   **Delivery Period:** 8 Weeks
*   **Unloading Responsibility:** Client/Department (SDSC SHAR) Scope
*   **Insurance:** Vendor/Supplier (Comprehensive risk coverage at supplier's own cost)
*   **Packing Requirements:** Sufficiently and properly packed for transport, at vendor's expense. Cable must be wound on strong wooden drums. Ends of cable sealed with rubber or PVC caps. Drum length tolerance: +5% / -2% of nominal length.

## Payment & Taxes
*   **Currency:** INR (Inferred)
*   **Payment Terms:** 100% payment after receipt and acceptance of materials on pro-rata basis.
*   **Taxes:** Taxes (GST, duties) to be indicated separately in percentage. If silent, rates are presumed inclusive of taxes & duties.

## Financial Security
*   **Performance BG:** 3% of the total value, valid till the warranty period plus 2 months grace.
*   **Security Deposit:** 3% of the contract price, valid till completion of total contractual obligation plus 90 days.
*   **EMD:** None

## Penalties
*   **Liquidated Damages:** 0.5% per week (Max: 10.0% of the undelivered Item)
*   **Warranty:** Minimum one year from the date of supply and acceptance.
